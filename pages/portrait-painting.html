Portrait Painting

Capture the Human Form

Learn anatomy, skin tones, and expressive techniques to create lifelike portraits.

Course Overview

Advanced

Duration: 12 Weeks

An advanced course for artists ready to tackle the complexity of the human face. We explore structure, emotion, and the subtle nuances that bring a portrait to life.

What You'll Learn:

  • Head Construction: Loomis and Asaro plane methods.
  • Facial Features: In-depth study of eyes, nose, mouth, and ears.
  • Skin Tones: Mixing realistic flesh tones for various lighting.
  • Expression: Capturing emotion and personality.
