Watercolor Mastery

Explore Fluidity & Color

Master the delicate and unpredictable nature of watercolors for breathtaking results.

Course Overview

Intermediate

Duration: 8 Weeks

Dive deep into watercolor techniques. Learn to control the water, embrace the happy accidents, and create luminous, transparent washes.

What You'll Learn:

  • Wet-on-Wet: Creating soft backgrounds and blends.
  • Wet-on-Dry: Adding sharp details and textures.
  • Color Mixing: Avoiding mud and keeping colors fresh.
  • Resist Techniques: Using masking fluid and wax.
