Drawing Fundamentals

Master the Basics of Drawing

Build a solid foundation in line, form, and composition to elevate your artistic skills.

Course Overview

Beginner

Duration: 8 Weeks

This comprehensive course is designed for absolute beginners and those looking to refresh their foundational skills. We cover everything from holding the pencil correctly to understanding complex perspective.

What You'll Learn:

  • Line & Shape: Understanding contour drawing and constructive forms.
  • Value & Shading: Creating 3D illusions with light and shadow.
  • Perspective: 1-point, 2-point, and 3-point perspective basics.
  • Composition: Rules of thirds, balanace, and focal points.
